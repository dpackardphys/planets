Are there microbes in the plumes of Enceladus?

Porco CC, Dones L, Mitchell C, Astrobiology Aug 2017, ahead of print
Title: "Could it be snowing microbes on Enceladus? Assessing Conditions in its plume and implications for future 
missions."
Cassini's exploration of the small saturnian moon Enceladus by both remote sensing and in situ investigations has demonstrated its status as a prime candidate for astrobiological study and the search for evidence of life.
